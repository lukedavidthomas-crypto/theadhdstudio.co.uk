Who we work with

Built for the people who need it most

We work with anyone who wants to genuinely support ADHD and neurodivergent users — not tick a box, but actually make a difference.

🏥
Healthcare Providers
Clinics, therapy practices, assessment services and care organisations. Apps that support patients between appointments, track symptoms, build routines or reduce the admin that comes with a diagnosis.
🏢
Businesses & Employers
HR teams and people-first organisations who want to better support neurodivergent staff. Tools for focus, task management, sensory check-ins, or onboarding support — built around your team's real needs.
🎓
Schools & Education
Primary schools, secondary schools, colleges and universities. Apps for students, teachers or both — whether that's a classroom focus tool, a homework planner, or a parent communication system.
🤝
Charities & Nonprofits

Individuals with an Idea

Digital Health Startups
Early-stage companies building in the mental health or neurodiversity space who need a development partner that truly understands their users — not just the tech stack.
What we can build

The only limit is your imagination

These are examples of what we've thought about, been asked about, or could build tomorrow. If yours isn't here, that doesn't matter — tell us what you need.

🧠
ADHD Symptom Tracker
Healthcare · Therapy · Self-management

A patient-facing app to log daily mood, focus levels, sleep, medication and task completion. Generates visual reports for clinicians ahead of appointments — turning vague "how have you been?" into real, useful data. Fully customisable to your clinical workflow and branding.

Patient app Clinician dashboard Reminders
🏢
Workplace Focus Companion
Employers · HR · Neurodiversity teams

A private, branded app for neurodivergent employees. Pomodoro-style focus sessions, task prioritisation, sensory environment check-ins, and a "what's overwhelming me right now?" brain dump that suggests one small next step. No data shared with the employer — trust by design.

Focus tools Private data Branded
🎓
Student Planner & Routine Builder
Schools · Colleges · Universities

An ADHD-first academic planner that breaks assignments into micro-steps, builds morning and evening routines, sends gentle reminders at the right times, and celebrates every completed step — not just the final grade. Can integrate with school timetables and parent/teacher visibility options.

Student-facing Routine builder Parent view
💊
Medication & Routine Adherence App
Healthcare · Pharmacy · Patient support

People with ADHD often struggle with consistent routines — including taking medication. A smart daily routine app with medication logging, configurable reminders, streak rewards and a "missed dose" protocol. All data stored privately on-device. Can be co-branded with a clinic or pharmacy.

Medication log Streak rewards On-device data
💰
ADHD-Friendly Financial Wellness App
Financial wellbeing · Employers · Charities

Beyond our own Counta app — a fully custom white-label financial wellbeing tool. Goal-based saving, spending pattern awareness, impulse-pause prompts before purchases, and visual progress that makes money feel manageable rather than overwhelming. Built around your users' financial context.

Goal saving White-label Custom branding
🗓️
Appointment & Transition Prep Tool
Healthcare · Social care · Support services

Getting to an appointment is half the battle for many ADHD patients. An app that sends multi-stage reminders, provides pre-appointment prep checklists, reduces the anxiety of what to expect, and logs how the appointment went. Can be customised for any kind of care or support service.

Smart reminders Anxiety reduction Custom workflows
🎮
Gamified Behaviour Support App
SEND schools · Care organisations · Parents

A rewards-based support app for children and young people with ADHD. XP points, unlockable badges, character customisation and daily challenges mapped to therapeutic goals — designed with the people who support them. Fully configurable reward structures and target behaviours.

XP & rewards Child-friendly Configurable goals
