🧠 About Us

Built by an ADHD team.

We didn't set out to build a productivity app. We set out to build something that actually worked for us — a neurodiverse team who'd tried every planner, every app, every system, and still felt behind.

Our story

The ADHD Studio is a small, neurodiverse app studio based in the UK. We're part of Tim Tom Studio, a web and app design agency — but The ADHD Studio is its own thing. A dedicated space to build tools that work the way ADHD brains actually work.

We got tired of productivity apps that made us feel worse. Apps that lectured us about discipline. Apps that assumed you could just sit down, make a list, and get through it. That's not how our brains work — and we knew it.

So we built ADH Do It. Not as a side project, but as something we genuinely needed. And then we published it. And then we built Counta, because tracking time and upcoming moments is half the battle with ADHD.

Why ADHD-specific apps matter

ADHD isn't a focus problem. It's a dopamine regulation problem. The brain struggles to generate enough internal motivation for tasks that don't have immediate rewards. That's why generic to-do apps fail — they ask ADHD brains to use a system designed for neurotypical ones.

ADHD-first design means:

  • Building in dopamine hits at every step — XP, confetti, streaks
  • Reducing decisions to zero — one action per screen
  • Solving time blindness, not ignoring it
  • Making progress visible and tangible, not just tracked
  • Never guilt-tripping, always encouraging
